Description

We use computer passwords every day, whether to access our email account, social networking sites, or even to do online banking. One of the challenges we may have when picking a password is making it easy for you to remember, but hard for other people to guess. It may not be a good idea, for example, to use our dog's name, our street address, or any information that's somehow connected to our username. For instance, if Mark Anthony uses the login "Manthony" to access his email and lives at 13 Maribyrnong street, the password "Maribyrnong13" might not be a good choice. Though it might be easy for him to remember, it is That is certainly much longer, it's not a word, and it does not have any identifiable information. But who can remember that? And if you write it down and misplace the piece of paper, that isn't really more secure.

A strong password is one that you can remember easily, but that is long, is made up of a couple of words, numbers and punctuation but doesn't have anything in it that someone would guess if they looked you up on the Internet. This application will be like a blessing for all those users who wants to know how strong their password is; that they have just created for their social media app (Facebook, twitter, email), whether a hacker will be able to crack his/her password.
